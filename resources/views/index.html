By Air
The closest airport is in Coimbatore, located about 27 kilometers from the campus. Most airlines operate flights to Coimbatore from all major cities in India.

By Bus
The nearest major bus terminals are at Gandhipuram (about 22 kms) and Ukkadam (about 18 kms). There are regular bus services from Gandhipuram (Bus Nos. 96, A3 and 3G) and Ukkadam (Bus Nos. 96, 48, S2, 3G and A3). Alight at Ettimadai Piruvu Bus Stop (Amrita College) and hire an auto rickshaw to the campus (3 kms). A3 bus will come upto the University Gate. The travel time from Ukkadam to Ettimadai Piruvu is around 45 minutes.

Ettimadai is a request stop for all Kerala and Tamil Nadu State Transport buses from Kerala. Alight at Ettimadai Piruvu Bus Stop (Amrita College) and hire an auto rickshaw to the campus (3 kms). Travel time from Palakkad to Ettimadai Piruvu is around one hour.

By Train
Passenger trains are available from Coimbatore Railway Station and Palghat Railway Station. Ettimadai Railway Station is only about 500 metres from the campus and one may easily walk to campus upon alighting at the station.

anokha

Dare to be Different

Anokha, the tech fest of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore, is the 3-day congregation of some of the brightest minds in India. Founded in 2010, Anokha has grown by leaps and bounds and has progressed to become one of the largest tech fests in South India. It witnesses a variety of events and workshops in different domains like Engineering, Business, Social Media, Finance, etc. Attractions like the talk series- Lumiére, Anokha's MUN edition- LeadersMeet, Anokha Expo and the pro-show night add to its charm.

The fest aims to bring the best out of students by providing them an invaluable opportunity to discover, develop and demonstrate their talents (and have a great time doing it). This year, we are determined to do the same... only bigger and better!




Amrita Viswa Vidyapeetham is a multi-campus, multi-disciplinary research university that is accredited 'A' by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). It is currently ranked among the top 30 Indian universities in the BRICS' International QS University Rankings, 2015.

Anokha 2016 will be one amongst the few techfests in India with accreditation and support from United Nations Academic Impact (UNAI), a United Nations initiative to align institutions of higher education, scholarship and research with the United Nations and with each other.

      • Eventide

        EVENTIDE, the entertainment hub of Anokha, has always kept its promise by enticing all with music, art and dance performances every year. Once again, we are back to keep that word. On these three eagerly awaited days of Anokha in the past, enjoyed the graceful twirls of Natyasudha- the Dance Club of Amrita, swayed to the melodies from Ragasudha, the Music Club, we have witnessed performances by well-known artists like RAM CHARI, SIVAMANI, STEPHEN DEVASSY, KARTHIK and BENNY DAYAL. This year for Eventide, we bring to you the musical maestros HARICHARAN and SHAKTHISREE. With over 1000 songs to their names, these Filmfare, Asianet and Vijay award winners are set to rock the stage on the last night of Anokha marking the end of the spectacular fest.


      LUMIÉRE


      The Lumiére Conclave

      The word "Lumiére" stands for "light" in French. The Lumiére conclave's mission as a premium talk series is to establish a forum that sheds light on the right things, to the right audience. The year 2015 saw the inaugural edition of The Lumiére conclave which had elite policy makers such as Dr. J.P.Narayanan and category-defining innovators such as Mr. Saurabh Kaushal, ex-founder of Xovian. This year Lumiére continues its journey as we prepare an ensemble of speakers from different avenues of Modern day India who change and influence India in a small way, every day. It will be a limited-admit event , taking place on the 19th of February. Our distinguished speakers will be introduced to our viewers over the next few days along with details about registration. So stay tuned to this page and the official Anokha facebook page to witness our roster unfold.

      LEADERSMEET

      LeadersMeet - Anokha's MUN


      "Never doubt that a small group of committed people can change the world.
      Indeed, it is the only thing that ever has."

      - Margaret Mead

      Gear up to get up and use your voice as a global citizen, to don the hat of diplomats, to discuss issues affecting various countries and to ponder over feasible solutions for crisis round the globe coz it is time for the LEADERSMEET, Amrita's very own MUN chapter. Be it Economics, Terrorism, Environment, Politics, Cross-border relations, Regionalism ─ we strive to cover everything in the councils and this will surely enlighten the minds of the students who come for this conference by helping them understand the role they can play to make this world a better place.
